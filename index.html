Frequently Asked Questions

What is Culture Kitchen?

Culture Kitchen is a vibrant dining experience that brings together flavors from around Nigeria. We offer a diverse menu that celebrates Nigerian cuisines, making every dish a unique culinary journey. Whether you're looking for traditional recipes or contemporary dishes, our chefs create exceptional meals that delight the senses.

How do I place an order?

You can place an order on our website by visiting the ‘Order Now’ section. Browse our menu, select your items, and proceed to checkout. Enter your delivery details and choose your preferred payment method. Once confirmed, your order will be processed and delivered promptly.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes, we offer catering services for events of all sizes. You can book our catering service by sending an inquiry to hello@culturekitchen.ng. Our team will be happy to discuss your requirements and customize a menu for your event.

Can I make a reservation online?

Absolutely! You can easily make a reservation sending a WhatsApp message to this number: +234 813 003 9255. Simply add your preferred date, time, and party size, and we'll ensure a table is ready for you. Walk-ins are also welcome, but making a reservation ensures you won’t have to wait for a table during peak hours.
